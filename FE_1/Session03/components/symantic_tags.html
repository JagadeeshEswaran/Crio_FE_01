Semantic Elements/Tags

  • <div> & <span> are non-semantic elements.
  • They do not describe anything about the content within them
  • Semantic elements clearly descrie the content within them.
  • These are "Block Elements"
  • Ex.: <section>, <heaader>, <footer>, <nav> & <article>
